  • face Who are we?

    Our goal is to save your time and help you build a high performing stock portfolio. We screen the world for significant insider buys on a daily basis and share the results with you for free. According to our research, this service will save you up to 20 hours a month in screening and research time.

  • new_releases Why what we do is important?

    Tracking significant insider buys is a great hunting ground for your next high performing investment. The rational is straightforward. Insiders might sell their shares for any number of reasons (buying a house, diversify, paying taxes etc.), but they buy them for only one: they think the underlying business is undervalued. .

  • timeline Do insiders outperform the market?

    It’s been empirically proven that insider buys have beaten the market indexes over the past 50 years. Professors Leslie A. Jeng and Andrew Metrick (2003) found that insider buys outperform the market by 6% a year. But apart from empirical research, it also makes intuitive sense. Insiders are better informed about the underlying business than the average investor, so it’s not weird to expect an overall outperformance.

  • visibility Why favour follow the insiders above traditional stock screeners?

    Conventional stock screeners use historical data to deliver a list of potential investment ideas. But investing is about the future, so we believe it's more useful to provide a list of ideas incorporating future assessments. And who is best at assessing the future of a business? Exactly, insiders buying their own company!

  • search What are the selection criteria for the search results?

    We only list significant insider buys. We define significant as purchases above $10’000 on a daily basis. Moreover, we only list transactions that are bought on the personal account of the insider. This implies we exclude purchases made indirectly by third parties.
